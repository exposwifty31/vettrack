You are a product marketing expert.

Your task is to create a real screen-recorded demo video (60–90 seconds) using the actual VetTrack app.

Do NOT create animations or simulations.
Use real app screens and real interactions.

⸻

Goal

Show a realistic veterinary technician workflow during a shift.

The video must demonstrate how the app solves:
	•	Lost equipment
	•	Lack of accountability
	•	Poor communication

This is NOT a feature walkthrough.
It is a story of real usage.

⸻

Recording Setup
	•	Record directly from a mobile device (preferred) or browser
	•	Use realistic demo data:
	•	Equipment names (e.g. “IV Pump #3”, “Monitor #2”)
	•	Rooms (ICU, Room 4, Surgery)
	•	Keep UI clean and readable
	•	Avoid shaky or slow interactions

⸻

Show confusion:

	•	“Where is the equipment?”

→ “Check Out”

⸻

	•	Show equipment assigned to:
	•	room or patient

⸻

	•	Tap “Report Issue”
	•	Add:
	•	note
	•	photo

“Clear issue reporting. No guesswork.”

	•	Tap:

⸻

⸻

	•	Keep cuts fast and smooth
